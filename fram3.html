MESCE is the M.E.S College of Engineering, a private, self-financing engineering institution in Kuttippuram, Kerala.

It is recognized by AICTE and CoA, offering undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, architecture, and management, and is affiliated with the University of Calicut. The college is noted for its quality education, comprehensive facilities including a digital library and campus-wide network, and an active student life with events like the annual MESTECH fest
